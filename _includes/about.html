About

Living with type one changes a person's body. We consume thousands and thousands of calories in low blood sugar supplies over the years. Bodies develop bruises and knots from injections and pump sites. Fingers become calloused from years of blood glucose checking. Diabetes can also inspire people to be more active or take better care of themselves in incredible ways. Regardless of any changes, I know I feel lucky to have an able, working body that can live off of synthetic hormones. We want to show people that type one bodies living with diabetes are diverse, beautiful, capable, and embraced.

Our Models

All the T1D Exposed participants strive to embrace their lives and bodies with type one diabetes every day.
